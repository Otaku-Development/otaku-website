Totos Privacy Policy

The short version: Totos collects nothing.

Last updated: 12 July 2026

Totos is a photo and video viewer designed for parents to share their photo library safely with toddlers. It is built so that your family's data never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Totos does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no user accounts, and no networking features of any kind.

Permissions the app uses

  • Photo library (read-only): used solely to display your photos and videos inside the app. Nothing is modified, uploaded, or shared.
  • Microphone and speech recognition (optional): used only if you enable Safe Word Protection. Audio is processed on your device by Apple's speech recognition to detect the safe words you configure. Recordings and transcripts are not stored by Totos and are never sent to us — we have no servers.

Children's privacy

Totos is intended to be used by children under a parent's supervision. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, no personal information about you or your children is ever gathered or processed by us.

Settings and preferences

Your preferences (date filter, volume, safe words, whether onboarding has been shown) are stored locally on your device and are removed when you delete the app.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of Totos ever changes any of the above, this policy will be updated and the change described in the App Store release notes before it takes effect.
