Burst

Hold the shutter, get thirty photos.
Burst turns them into one loop.

Burst photos, Live Photos, and short videos become looping GIFs and MP4s — made on your iPhone, never uploaded anywhere.

Four frames, a third of a second apart — the kind of sequence sitting in your camera roll right now. The last cell is the loop Burst makes from them.

How it works

PICK
Choose any burst, Live Photo, or video Burst finds everything animatable in your library and shows it in one grid.
TUNE
Loop it forward, in reverse, or bounce it Set the speed from 0.5× to 2× and watch the loop update live — what you see is exactly what you export.
SHARE
Send it anywhere Export as GIF and drop it into Messages, WhatsApp, or Slack — there's an iMessage extension for sending loops without leaving the conversation.
