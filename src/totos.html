Totos

Your photo library, turned into a toddler-proof picture book.

Totos gives little ones a big, friendly, colourful viewer for your photos and videos — with none of the danger. Everything is read-only, there is nothing to buy, share or delete, and all the settings live safely behind a parent verification puzzle.

What makes it safe

  • Read-only, always — photos and videos can be looked at, never changed or deleted.
  • Date filter — only show photos taken after a date you choose; older memories stay private.
  • Parent gate — settings are locked behind a puzzle pre-readers can't solve.
  • Safe Word Protection — say your chosen word and the screen instantly goes black.
  • Completely private — no accounts, no ads, no analytics, no internet features.

Frequently asked questions

How do I open the settings?

Tap Parents on the home screen, then solve the puzzle: three numbers are shown as words (for example “four two seven”) — type them as digits (427).

The screen has gone black — how do I get back?

That's Safe Word Protection doing its job. Tap the top-right corner of the screen five times, solve the parent puzzle, then tap Resume.

Why can't my child see all of our photos?

The date filter is on. In Settings, change “Show photos from” to an earlier date and the grids update immediately.

How do I stop my toddler leaving the app?

Use iOS Guided Access: turn it on in the iPhone's Settings under Accessibility, then triple-click the side button while Totos is open.
